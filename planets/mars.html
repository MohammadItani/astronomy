Mars

Red Planet

  • 1

    Mars revolves around itself every 24 hours, once like the earth, but its rotation around the sun takes place in a period of 787 days.

  • 2

    Its distance from the sun is 142 million miles, and its temperature during the day is a few degrees above zero, but at night it descends to 70 degrees below zero and its surface is land with no sea or water in it.

  • 3

    Its air is composed of a gas heavier than oxygen, and its gravity is one-third of the Earth's gravity and is not sufficient to preserve the oxygen in its air, for these reasons it is not suitable for life at all.
