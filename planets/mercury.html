Mercury

The Swift Planet

  • 1

    Mercury revolves around itself and around the sun, in a period of 88 days.

  • 2

    One of its sides is always directed towards the sun, so half of it is sun and half of it is blooming.

  • 3

    Its density is about half the density of the Earth, so there is little gravity. And there is no air in it, for these reasons it is not suitable for life.
